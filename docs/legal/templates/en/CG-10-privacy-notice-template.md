## Privacy Notice — AI-Assisted Support

### Who we are

[Customer company name] ("we", "us") provides customer support for [product name]. We use NestFleet, an AI-assisted support platform, to help process and respond to your support requests.

### What data we collect

When you contact our support team, we collect:
- Your email address and name (as provided in your message)
- The content of your support request and any follow-up messages
- Technical metadata: timestamps, channel (email/chat), message identifiers

### How we use your data

Your support data is processed for the following purposes:
- **Triage and classification**: AI agents automatically classify your request by type and severity to ensure it reaches the right team member quickly
- **Response drafting**: For straightforward issues, AI agents may draft a response based on our knowledge base. All AI-generated responses are clearly disclosed
- **Change management**: If your request involves a software issue, it may result in a tracked change request and code fix
- **Knowledge improvement**: Resolved cases may be used to improve our internal knowledge base for future support quality

### AI involvement disclosure

Some responses to your support requests are generated or assisted by AI. These are clearly marked with a disclosure statement. You can request human-only handling at any time by replying with "HUMAN" to any AI-generated message.

### Lawful basis

We process your support data based on:
- **Contractual necessity** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): processing is necessary to provide the support service included in our product agreement
- **Legitimate interest** (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)): improving our support quality and product reliability

### Data retention

Support case data is retained for [configurable: default 365 days] after case closure, then automatically deleted. You can request earlier deletion at any time.

### Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:
- **Access** your personal data (Art. 15)
- **Rectification** of inaccurate data (Art. 16)
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten") (Art. 17)
- **Restriction** of processing (Art. 18)
- **Data portability** (Art. 20)
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21)

To exercise any of these rights, contact: [customer DPO/privacy contact email]

### Data processing location

Your support data is processed on [Customer's infrastructure location — e.g., "our servers in Frankfurt, Germany"]. AI processing uses [Customer's chosen LLM provider — e.g., "OpenAI API" or "self-hosted model"]. No support data is shared with the NestFleet vendor.

### No automated decision-making

NestFleet does not make automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects on you. All consequential actions require human approval. AI agents propose responses; human team members approve actions that affect your account or service.
